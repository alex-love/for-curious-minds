What is For Curious Minds?

This website is dedicated to my dad who’s name I’ll omit out of respect for his love of privacy. One day I’ll write more about him but I’ll tell you a few things. He left me his Kindle library, he had me and my siblings in a group chat called “For Curious Minds”, and he was never afraid to tell you how he felt. He passed away from a brain injury about 4 years ago and his mind is what I’ll miss most about him.
